### 3. 触觉反馈增强 (Haptic Feedback)

**新增文件**:
- `DreamHapticFeedback.swift` (370 行)

**功能详情**:
- ✅ `DreamHapticType` 枚举 - 35+ 种反馈类型：
  - 基础反馈 (5 种): lightImpact, mediumImpact, heavyImpact, softImpact, rigidImpact
  - 通知反馈 (3 种): success, error, warning
  - 交互反馈 (10+ 种): selection, toggleSwitch, sliderAdjustment, starRating 等
  - 场景反馈 (15+ 种): recording, refresh, load, drag, modal 等
  - 特殊反馈：unlockAchievement, biometricSuccess, cameraShutter 等

- ✅ 服务功能：
  - `trigger(_:)` - 触发指定类型反馈
  - `triggerSequence(_:delay:)` - 连续反馈
  - `triggerRampUpFeedback(count:baseType:)` - 强度递增
  - `triggerRampDownFeedback(count:baseType:)` - 强度递减
  - `setEnabled(_:)` - 启用/禁用
  - `setIntensity(_:)` - 设置强度 (0.0-1.0)
  - `testFeedback()` - 测试反馈

- ✅ 设置支持：
  - `@AppStorage("hapticEnabled")` - 开关
  - `@AppStorage("hapticIntensity")` - 强度
